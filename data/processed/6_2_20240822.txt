**Title:**
{'Unemployment Insurance Weekly Claims: Week Ending August 17, 2024'}

**Overview:**
||This report provides the unemployment insurance claims data for the week ending August 17, 2024. The 4-week moving average for initial claims decreased slightly to 236,000.
